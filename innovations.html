360Fashion and Tech Innovation Awards

November 18, 2015 by Eve-Rosemarie


I had the chance to attend to 360 Fashion and Tech Innovation Awards on November 3rd at City View Metreon in San Francisco.

This event was a visionary design exhibition based on technology keywords by apparel design and merchandising students at San Francisco State University.

The main speakers and guests were :

Sandy Carter, IBM General Manager.
Paolo Borges of Styliff , explained how IBM Cloud technologies let Styliff deliver a mobile dressing room in global markets.
Lisa Lang of ElektroCouture , described why she’s transitioning to IBM Cloud and Bluemix before the next Fashion Week.
A lot of startups were there, using IBM Cloud technology in their innovative products.


More about the link between Fashion and Technology introduced during this event :
Technology have never been so closed to the skin (Apple watch, Google glass…). Fashion was introduced as the way you present yourself to the world. Nowadays, Fashion is a strong way of communication, it allows an personal identification and a personal positioning.


More about the topic of personal Data in the industry :
Using the User Experience in the restaurant : what if a CRM allows your industry to know about the tastes and the habits of your users ? What if your restaurant could suggest to customers what others with the same tates have already ordered and enjoyed ?
Data can be used to increase the experience’s quality of your users : schedule’s habits, favorite flavours ….


All the attendees shared their views on technology innovation, visionary design, and fashion.
